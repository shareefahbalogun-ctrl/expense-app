Help / FAQ

Everything you need to know about using the Ultimate Expense Tracker

What is the Dashboard?
The Dashboard is your main overview of finances. It shows:
  • Total Balance
  • Total Income
  • Total Expenses
  • Monthly Budget and Remaining Budget
  • Quick links to Weekly Goals, Budget Alerts, and Financial Snapshot
Tip: You can quickly see if you are over budget by checking the remaining budget bar.
How do I add Transactions?
You can add transactions via the sidebar or the "Add Transaction" button. Fill in:
  • Description
  • Amount
  • Category
  • Type (Income or Expense)
  • Optional Recurring settings
Tip: Use recurring transactions for regular bills to save time.
What are Analytics / Charts?
Analytics display visual charts of your finances:
  • Income vs Expenses
  • Category breakdown
  • Monthly budget progress
Tip: Check charts weekly to track trends and adjust budgets.
What are the Down Cards (Spending Pattern, Trend Comparison, Insights)?
These sections provide deeper insights:
  • Spending Pattern: Shows where your money goes
  • Trend Comparison: Compare past and current expenses
  • Insights: Tips based on your financial habits
  • Cash Flow: Visualize income vs expenses over time
  • Future Projection: Predict budget for upcoming months
Tip: Use the Future Projection card to plan your upcoming expenses effectively.
How do I Export or Import my data?
Export: Click "Export" to download a backup file.
Import: Use the "Import" button to upload a previous backup.
  • Always export before making major changes
  • Imported files must match the app format
  • Importing overwrites existing data
Tip: Keep a monthly backup to avoid data loss.
How do Notifications work?
The app can notify you about:
  • Transaction Reminders: Reminds you to log transactions
  • Budget Alerts: Warns when you approach or exceed your budget
  • Income Alerts: Notifies when new income is added
Tip: Enable only notifications you need to avoid clutter.
How do I customize Settings?
Settings let you adjust:
  • Appearance: Dark Mode, Font Size, Confetti
  • Currency & Date Format
  • Notifications: Enable/disable reminders and alerts
  • Budget & Savings Goals
  • Security: App PIN, Biometric login, 2FA
Tip: Remember to click "Save Settings" after making changes.
